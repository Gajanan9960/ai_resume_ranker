The Problem We Solve

Recruiters spend an average of 6 seconds scanning a single resume, and large hiring drives generate hundreds of applications. Manual screening is slow, inconsistent, and biased. Qualified candidates slip through the cracks while recruiters are overwhelmed.

AI Resume Ranker automates the screening step — using semantic NLP and skill-matching to objectively score every resume against the job description, so your team focuses only on the candidates who matter.

How It Works

📋

1. Paste the Job Description

Choose from popular roles or enter a custom job description and required skills for the position you are hiring for.

📤

2. Upload Resumes

Upload one or many resumes in PDF or image format. The system extracts text automatically — no manual copy-paste needed.

🤖

3. AI Scores & Ranks

Our engine uses Sentence-BERT semantic similarity and keyword matching to score each resume and assign a status: Top Candidate, Shortlisted, Review, or Rejected.

🎯

4. Review & Act

Filter, sort, and review ranked candidates on your dashboard. Send personalised emails directly, or export results to Excel/CSV for your team.
