About AI Resume Ranker

An intelligent tool built to take the pain out of resume screening

🎯 The Problem

Every open role attracts dozens — sometimes hundreds — of applications. Recruiters and hiring managers spend enormous amounts of time reading through resumes manually, leading to:

  • Inconsistent evaluation — different reviewers judge resumes differently
  • Slow time-to-hire — bottlenecks in the screening step delay the entire pipeline
  • Missed talent — strong candidates are overlooked when reviewers are fatigued
  • Bias — unconscious patterns affect who gets shortlisted

💡 Our Solution

AI Resume Ranker automates the first-pass screening step using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. It reads every resume in seconds, measures how well each candidate matches the job description, and produces an objective ranked list — so your team can focus its human judgement on the candidates who actually deserve a closer look.

⚙️ How the Scoring Works

Each resume receives a composite score built from four components:

  1. Keyword Match (40%) — How many of the required skills and keywords appear in the resume.
  2. Semantic Similarity (40%) — How closely the resume's meaning aligns with the job description, measured using Sentence-BERT embeddings. This catches synonyms and related concepts that simple keyword matching misses.
  3. Keyword Density (10%) — Whether the matched keywords appear naturally throughout the text rather than being crammed in once.
  4. Resume Quality (10%) — A length and completeness signal based on word count.

Based on the final score, each candidate is automatically tagged: Top Candidate (≥80%), Shortlisted (≥65%), Review (≥45%), or Rejected (<45%).

🚀 Key Features

  • Multi-format support: Accepts PDF and image resumes (JPG, PNG) with automatic text extraction.
  • Skill Gap Report: Shows matched skills and highlights missing ones for each candidate.
  • Recruiter Dashboard: Real-time statistics — total candidates, average score, high-potential rate, and interview-ready count.
  • One-click candidate email: Send tailored status emails (offer, shortlist, review, or rejection) directly from the dashboard.
  • Export: Download filtered results as Excel or CSV for sharing with your team.
  • Private & isolated: Your resumes and data are visible only to your account.

🛠️ Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python, Flask
  • NLP / AI: Sentence-BERT (all-MiniLM-L6-v2), spaCy (en_core_web_lg)
  • Resume Parsing: pdfminer.six (PDF), pytesseract + Pillow (images)
  • Database: MongoDB Atlas
  • Export: pandas, XlsxWriter
  • Frontend: Jinja2 templates, vanilla CSS
